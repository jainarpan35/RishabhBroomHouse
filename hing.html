The Legacy of Purity

Wagad Ki Pehli Pasand

Sudha Hing is a trusted name for premium Hing (Asafoetida), sourced from the finest farms and processed with utmost care. For over a decade, we've been the heart of kitchens in Banswara and beyond, delivering an aroma that defines authentic Indian cooking.

Our commitment is to purity. No adulteration, no fillers—just 100% premium grade Asafoetida that enhances flavor and aids digestion, rooted in Ayurvedic wisdom.
