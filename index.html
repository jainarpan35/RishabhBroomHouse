About Us

Founded in 2009 in Banswara, Rajasthan, Rishabh Broom House is a pioneer in the art of broom making. We are committed to sustainability, using natural grass and traditional skills to create eco-friendly, long-lasting brooms. Our brand is trusted by households, wholesalers, and retailers across India.

Why Choose Our Brooms?

Crafted with care, our brooms are:

  • Durable: Built to last, even under daily use
  • Eco-Friendly: 100% natural and biodegradable
  • Comfortable: Easy to grip and lightweight
  • Versatile: Available in compact, standard, and long-handle designs
